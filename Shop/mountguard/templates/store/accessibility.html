Accessibility Policy

We are committed to ensuring that our AI-driven e-commerce website is accessible to all users, including those with disabilities. This Accessibility Policy outlines the measures we have taken to improve the accessibility of our website.

1. Accessibility Standards

We aim to comply with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 to ensure our website is accessible to users with a range of disabilities.

2. Features to Enhance Accessibility

Our website includes the following accessibility features:

  • Text alternatives for non-text content, such as images and videos.
  • Keyboard navigable menus and interactive elements.
  • Responsive design to ensure usability on various devices and screen sizes.
  • Readable and scalable text.
  • Consistent and predictable navigation.

3. Feedback and Assistance

We welcome feedback on the accessibility of our website. If you encounter any accessibility issues or need assistance, please contact us.

4. Continuous Improvement

We are committed to continuously improving the accessibility of our website. We regularly review and update our accessibility practices to ensure we meet the needs of all users.

If you have any questions or concerns about our Accessibility Policy, please contact us.
